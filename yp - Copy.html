WHY JOIN YP?
  • Members of this community are interested in elevating their professional image, expanding their global network, connecting with peers locally.
  • The most important action for Young Professionals is to promote the concept at the local level via non-technical groups called affinity groups.
  • Young Professionals have embraced this concept and formalized affinity groups worldwide.
